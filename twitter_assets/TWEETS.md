## Tweet Templates & Examples

Here are a few different types of tweets to schedule or use for launch and ongoing engagement:

*Goal: Get people to understand the concept instantly.*
> 11 rings made Bill Russell immortal.
> 
> I built a game where you draft the perfect NBA player from real attributes—Curry's shooting, Wemby's size, Jordan's clutch—and try to win 12. 
> 
> Can you go a perfect 12-0 in the Finals? 
> Play free: 12-0.me 🏆👇
> 
> *(Attach a GIF of drafting a player and the final 12-0 screen)*

### 2. The "If you like X" (Riding trends)
*Goal: Piggyback off existing audiences.*
> If you liked 82-0, try 12-0.
> 
> Instead of drafting a team for one season, you draft a single god-tier player from historical attributes and simulate his entire career.

> 12-0.me

> *(Attach a screenshot of a funny or overpowered built player)*

*Goal: Get retweets from developers and indie makers.*

> It's free, no signup. Try to go 12-0: 12-0.me 🏀
